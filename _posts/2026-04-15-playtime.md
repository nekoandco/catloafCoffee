You called me pretty,  
so I sent you pictures of my body.  

I wanted intimacy,  
instead all I found was pleasure.  

It’s fun playing with you,  
but it’s not enough.  

1’s and 0’s are no substitute for the real thing.  

So I’ll keep making myself pretty,  
in the hopes that one day I can meet her.  

But until then,  
I’d like to keep playing with you.
